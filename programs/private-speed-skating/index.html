Private Speed Skating Lessons

Looking to improve your skills on the track or refine your skating technique? Private lessons provide a focused, personalized experience to help you achieve your speed skating goals.
Sessions are tailored to your skill level, whether you’re a beginner or an experienced skater. Lessons are one hour and can accommodate up to two people.


Contact us for more information or to schedule your private lesson!
